# 🏛️ Nyonya Digital Heritage: Echoes of the Straits
> **An Advanced HCI Prototype Bridging 19th-Century Aesthetics with 21st-Century Code**

[![University](https://img.shields.io/badge/University-Nottingham%20Malaysia-red)](https://www.nottingham.edu.my/)

---

## ▌ 01. PROJECT VISION
**Nyonya Digital Heritage** is not merely a web archive; it is a **Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) experiment** designed to solve the problem of "Historical Flattening" in digital museums. By transforming static physical artifacts—19th-century Peranakan Majolica tiles—into responsive, narrative-driven entities, this project explores how **Presence** and **Digital Tactility** can be engineered in a browser environment.

> "A tile without a footstep is just cold stone. An interface without human empathy is dead code." — *Project Philosophy*

---

## ▌ 02. CORE RESEARCH FEATURES

### 🖱️ 1. HCI Design Affordances
* **Tactile Visual Feedback:** Implemented complex CSS transformation matrices that provide subtle luminous "glow" and brightness shifts upon interaction, simulating the reflective nature of glazed ceramic.
* **Chrono-Lighting System:** A dedicated **Day/Night mode** allows researchers to observe how ambient digital lighting affects user immersion and the perceived "soul" of the artifact.

### 📜 2. Heuristic Narrative Engine
* **Branching Storylines:** Utilizing a multi-nodal logic system, users engage in a "dialogue" with tiles. Each choice leads to different historical perspectives: the artisan’s struggle, the merchant’s wealth, or the matriarch’s secrets.
* **Multilingual Context:** Designed to accommodate the linguistic hybridity of the Straits (Hokkien, Malay, English), reflecting the developer’s own linguistic research.

### 🏺 3. High-Fidelity Artifact Gallery
* **Dynamic Content Loading:** Efficiently manages a collection of authentic high-resolution imagery (`1.png` to `5.png`).
* **Interactive Palette:** (Conceptualized) Allowing for the exploration of color symbolism in Peranakan culture.

## ▌ 04. RESEARCH ALIGNMENT
This prototype is specifically designed to align with the research goals of the **Digital Heritage Centre (UNNC)** and **HCI Labs (UNM)**:
* **No-Code Authoring:** Exploring how AI-assisted "Vibe Coding" can empower cultural curators to build their own digital experiences.
* **Maker Education:** Demonstrating a "Thinker-Maker" approach by bridging hard-coded logic with artistic narrative.
